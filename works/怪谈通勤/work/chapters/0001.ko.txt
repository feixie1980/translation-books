I mean truly obsessed. Beyond just thinking, "Oh, this is fun," to the point where it leaves such a deep impression that you willingly pour extra time and money into it.

Watching the director's cut of a movie, buying the official artbook for a game, paying for early-access previews of a webtoon, or going to a singer's concert...
If you take it a step further, another great example would be buying merchandise. Yes, what people usually call "merch."
Figurines, slogan banners, badges, plushies...
I can say with absolute certainty that I have never spent a single penny on things like that in my entire life.
Until now, that is.
This is a department store.
The wildly popular pop-up store had already finished distributing all its waiting tickets the exact moment the department store opened at 10:00 AM.
And against all odds, I managed to get one of those tickets.
"The 2:30 PM slot is now open for entry! Please come this way!"
"Ah, finally!"
"Hey, let's hurry!"

'2:30 PM... Yep, that's me.'

"Wait..."
"Is he a reseller?"
"Isn't he a professional scalper?"

[Prophecy of the Apocalypse: Dark Exploration Records]
